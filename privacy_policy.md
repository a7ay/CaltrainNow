## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how CaltraiNow ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to respecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

---

## Information We Collect

Our App does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

We do collect limited, non-personal information automatically when you use the App:

### Usage Analytics

We collect anonymous usage data to understand how users interact with the App and to improve its functionality. This may include information such as which features are used, how often the App is opened, session duration, and general interaction patterns. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you personally.

### Crash and Performance Data

We use a third-party crash reporting service (such as Crashlytics or Sentry) to collect diagnostic data when the App crashes or experiences errors. This may include device model, operating system version, the state of the App at the time of the crash, and stack traces. This information is used solely to identify and fix bugs and improve App stability.

---

## Information We Do Not Collect

To be clear, our App does **not** collect any of the following:

- Names, email addresses, or other personally identifiable information
- Location data
- Photos, files, or documents
- Payment or financial information
- Contacts or call logs

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- **To improve the App:** Usage analytics help us understand which features are most valuable and where we can make improvements.
- **To fix bugs and maintain stability:** Crash reports allow us to identify, diagnose, and resolve technical issues.

We do not use your information for advertising, marketing, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to improving the App's functionality and performance.

## Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services that may collect information on our behalf:

- **Crash Reporting (e.g., Crashlytics / Sentry):** Collects diagnostic and device information when the App crashes. These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review:
  - Firebase Crashlytics: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  - Sentry: https://sentry.io/privacy/

These third-party services are used strictly for crash reporting and diagnostics. We do not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or any other third parties.
