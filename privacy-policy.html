Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how [Your App Name] ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to respecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

Information We Collect

Our App does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number. We collect limited, non-personal information automatically when you use the App.

Usage Analytics

We collect anonymous usage data to understand how users interact with the App and to improve its functionality. This may include which features are used, how often the App is opened, session duration, and general interaction patterns. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you personally.

Crash & Performance Data

We use a third-party crash reporting service (such as Crashlytics or Sentry) to collect diagnostic data when the App encounters errors. This may include device model, operating system version, the state of the App at the time of the crash, and stack traces. This information is used solely to identify and fix bugs and improve App stability.

Information We Do Not Collect

To be clear, our App does not collect any of the following:

  • Names, email addresses, or other personally identifiable information
  • Location data
  • Photos, files, or documents
  • Payment or financial information
  • Contacts or call logs

How We Use Your Information

  • To improve the App: Usage analytics help us understand which features are most valuable and where we can make improvements.
  • To fix bugs and maintain stability: Crash reports allow us to identify, diagnose, and resolve technical issues.
We do not use your information for advertising, marketing, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to improving the App.

Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services that may collect information on our behalf:

Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your data:
